Declaration of Independence
Detailed Description

Following the military crackdown by Pakistani forces on the night of March 25, 1971, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman declared the independence of Bangladesh in the early hours of March 26, 1971.

This document represents the official declaration that formed the legal basis for the Bangladesh Liberation War and the subsequent establishment of the sovereign nation of Bangladesh.

Publication Date: March 26, 1971

Source: Bangladesh Government Liberation War Documents

Document ID: MJP/1971/001
